About Us

Dough Re Mi began as a small home-based bakery with a passion for creating fresh and delicious baked goods for family and friends. Over time, our love for baking and commitment to quality allowed us to grow into a trusted local bakery in Trinidad and Tobago. We take pride in using quality ingredients and traditional techniques, while also adding a modern touch to our recipes. Today, Dough Re Mi continues to serve the community with a wide variety of breads, pastries, and sweet treats, all made with care, creativity, and a dedication to excellence.

Mission

To craft fresh, high-quality baked goods that delight every customer, combining passion, creativity, and traditional baking techniques.

Vision

To become the leading bakery in Trinidad and Tobago.
